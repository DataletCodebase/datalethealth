Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2026

DataletHealthcareApp ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized health and wellness experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile details provided during signup.
  • Health Data: Information you provide regarding your medical conditions, prescriptions, diet, sleep, and vitals.
  • Location Data: We request access to your location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) to provide personalized health data and services near you.
  • Camera and Media: We request access to your camera and photo library to allow you to upload profile pictures and medical documents (CAMERA, READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_IMAGES).
  • Audio: Access to the microphone for voice-based interactions or recording audio (RECORD_AUDIO).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Personalize your experience (e.g., diet and activity recommendations).
  • Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support.
  • Ensure compliance with medical standards and safety.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or mobile devices is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You can manage your permissions (Location, Camera, etc.) through your device settings or within the app's settings menu. You may also update your profile information or request account deletion at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
